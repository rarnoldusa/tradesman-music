Welcome to Tradesman Music.

Tradesman Music is an independent music label and creative publishing project founded by Roy Arnold.

The label develops original music and fictional recording artists across multiple genres.

The first featured artist is Hendrix Green, a fictional Christian R&B/Soul recording artist whose music tells stories of faith, redemption, perseverance, and hope.

Additional artists coming soon!
